# Privacy Policy
**Qwen Explain Firefox** — Last updated: April 2026

## Overview
This extension is built with privacy as a priority. It does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing activity, or telemetry. All logic runs locally in your browser.

## Data Collection
**We collect zero data.**
- No URLs, selected text, or browsing history is logged or sent anywhere by this extension.
- No analytics, crash reporters, or tracking pixels are included.
- No cookies, device fingerprints, or identifiers are generated.

## Why Permissions Are Requested
- `contextMenus` — Adds "Explain in Qwen" to right-click menus.
- `tabs` & `scripting` — Opens a Qwen tab and injects your prompt into the input field after explicit user action.
- `clipboardWrite` & `notifications` — Fallback if auto-fill fails.
- `storage` — Saves your custom prompt and wait-time locally on your device.

## Third-Party Interaction
The extension communicates with [chat.qwen.ai](https://chat.qwen.ai/) **only** when you explicitly click the context menu item. The request is sent directly from your browser to Qwen's official site. We are not affiliated with Qwen and recommend reviewing their privacy policy separately.
